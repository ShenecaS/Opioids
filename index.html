Opioid Overdoses Continue to Rampage the Country

The opioid pandemic has resulted in thousands of deaths throughout the country. Usually, the cause is through pharmaceuticals prescribing medications such as Oxycotin to patients without discussing the addictive side effects. This map shows the top states with high overdose rates, the greyed out ones are states with extremely low overdose rates. States such as Virginia and Maine suffer with the highest rates. Settlements have taken place with Purdue Pharma, Pfizer, Merck and more. The highest being $4.25 billion dollars from Merck for a liability on the heart risks from their arthritis medication, Vioxx. According to the National Academy of Medicine, they’ve partnered with hundreds of non-profits and organizations “to reversing national trends in opioid misuse and overdose,” according to their website.
